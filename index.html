About

I build AI-powered automation systems and agent platforms. My focus is on creating intelligent workflows that connect LLMs with real-world tools — from task orchestration to autonomous agents that operate across Telegram, Notion, and GitHub.

Based on a philosophy that AI should handle the routine so humans can focus on what matters. I work primarily with TypeScript, Python, and the Claude ecosystem.
